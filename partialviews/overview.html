Welcome to PasswordManager

Why use a PasswordManager ?
  • Everything is either
    1. Hashed
    2. Encrypted
    3. Bi-crypted
    4. Salted
  • Not having to remember multiple passwords
  • Works on osx and windows
  • You only have to remember one main password
  • Android app incase you need your credentials to log in on your mobile device

Why did i make a PasswordManager when there are already hundreds of them out?

  1. I wanted to play around with the electron framework and have always wanted to make my own password managing application so, i thought, why not?
